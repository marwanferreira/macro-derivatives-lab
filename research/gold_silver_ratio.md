# Gold / Silver Ratio Trade

## Trade type
Cross-commodity futures spread

## CME instruments
- Gold futures (GC)
- Silver futures (SI)

## Direction
To be determined from pre-trade diagnostics:
- long gold / short silver
- or long silver / short gold

## Core thesis
Gold may outperform silver in a geopolitical and inflation-sensitive macro regime because gold behaves more like a safe haven, while silver remains more exposed to industrial and supply-chain dynamics.

## Pre-trade checks
- Gold/silver ratio level
- 20D / 60D / 120D z-score
- Percentile of current ratio
- Rolling correlation
- Hedge ratio estimation
- Macro consistency with trade direction

## Entry condition
Only enter if the ratio looks statistically stretched and the macro backdrop supports the chosen direction.

## Risks
- Silver squeeze
- Sharp change in industrial sentiment
- Gold underperforming despite macro stress
- Correlation breakdown
