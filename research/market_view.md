# Market View

## Core macro regime
Current market conditions appear to be driven by the following chain:

Iran / Hormuz tension -> oil pressure -> inflation fears -> rates repricing -> effects on gold, silver, and broader market sentiment.

## Main implications
- Rates volatility may stay elevated
- The Treasury curve may reprice non-linearly
- Oil remains a key macro shock variable
- Gold and silver may diverge enough to create relative-value opportunities

## Main themes to monitor
- Oil price moves and supply disruption headlines
- Treasury yield reactions
- Gold/silver relative performance
- Option implied volatility in rates, oil, and silver
